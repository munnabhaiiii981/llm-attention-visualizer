# Sample texts for testing attention patterns

## Simple Sentence
The cat sat on the mat.

## Pronoun Resolution  
John gave Mary the book. She thanked him for it.

## Complex Sentence
The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog while the sun sets beautifully over the distant mountains.

## Question
What is the capital of France and why is it important for European politics?

## Attention Pattern Test
Alice told Bob that she would meet him at the restaurant. He said he would be there.

## Technical Text
Machine learning models use attention mechanisms to focus on relevant parts of the input sequence.

## Story Fragment
The old wizard looked at the young apprentice. His eyes twinkled with ancient wisdom as he prepared to share the secret.
